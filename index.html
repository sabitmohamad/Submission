Commitment

Having a commitment is one way to get rich. When someone does something half-heartedly, then this will make him stagnant and ordinary. In order to be successful at something one does, one must be fully committed in intentions and actions. You also should not just give up if your efforts have not shown results, but you must continue to fight as a form of your commitment to something you are doing.

Clear Goal

For example, you want to be a successful entrepreneur, then start determining when that goal will be achieved at 20% and when you are at 50%. The important thing to do next is to carry out a predetermined plan. Unclear goals will make you procrastinate on tasks and work that should be done. Breaking down goals into simpler milestones will allow a person to celebrate small victories along the way to success. This of course makes you much more motivated. To become a successful person or become rich you have to write down the goals to be achieved and also the deadlines to achieve them.

Evaluation

One of the most common mistakes people make is that people don't measure their progress. From now on whatever goals you have, start evaluating and asking yourself and track and measure progress immediately. Measuring progress lets you know what is working and which areas need further improvement.

Focus on the Process not the Result

If all this time you have always focused on results only, you should start now to focus on the process to achieve results in the future. This kind of method would allow one to maintain motivation to achieve a goal. When you focus too much on the results that will be obtained, your motivation can decrease because you see the expected results are not immediately obtained and eventually stop the process.

Make it Habit

In addition to measuring the progress of the process, start to make good habits something you do every day. For example, you intend to be skilled in a certain skill area. Then, start to get used to studying and taking courses in the field. No need to feel burdened in learning something new. Instead, think of the activity as a new habit that is natural for you do it every day. That way you will live it more easily.
